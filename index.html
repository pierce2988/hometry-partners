Become a H°METRY Partner

H°METRY helps homebuyers understand their mortgage readiness, estimate payments, and connect with trusted local mortgage professionals — like you.

What is H°METRY
Mortgage readiness, made clear

H°METRY is an educational platform that scores a buyer's mortgage readiness, estimates real monthly payments with PMI, and helps them prepare before they ever talk to a lender. By the time they reach you, they understand their numbers.

📊

Readiness Score

Buyers get a 0–100 score across credit, DTI, and assets — so they know exactly where they stand.

🏦

Real Payment Math

Accurate payment breakdowns with PMI, taxes, and insurance across multiple down-payment scenarios.

🤝

Trusted Matching

When a buyer is ready, we connect them with vetted local mortgage professionals in their market.
